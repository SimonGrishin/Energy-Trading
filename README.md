## Energy Trading

### Summary
This development project is meant to showcase the different open-source methods utilized to build production level data pipelines, back-testing methods, and foundational trading strategies across energy trading.

These foundations support the development of propriatary trading models, short and long-term strategies, and high-frequency algorithms that can consistently produce alpha. 

### Goal
Utilizing multiple trading strategies and algorithms to accurately predict: **price shifts**, **volatility**, **capacity constraints**, and **price spikes**. The combination of models across asset classes will provide statistical and algorithmic experimentation to develop overall trading strategies to price the energy markets.

### Structure:
The repository is divided into *Data Pipelines*, *Backtesting*, *Trading*, and multiple alternative asset classes that are common in Energy Trading: *Crude Oil*, *Natural Gas*, *Coal*, *Renewables*, and *Capacity Markets*. Each of these sections has their own *data* and *models* to test and run the models independently. Then the best versions of each asset class are combined to form a energy trading strategy and possible dashboard to support timely decision making and consistent alpha generation. The methods in *Data Pipelines* and *Backtesting* will help maintain and support accurate predictions.
